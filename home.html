About The Doctor

Meet Dr. Panchshil Patel

Dr. Panchshil Patel has been practicing medicine for the past 10 years. Initially, he received his medical training at Ross University School of Medicine. He completed his residency at University of Connecticut School of Medicine – primary care internal medicine track.

He worked as an attending physician in medicine at Grady Memorial Hospital working for Emory University school of medicine. Here, he taught new medical doctors in training as well as medical students as an instructor of medicine and later assistant professor of medicine.

Dr. Patel has taken care of patients throughout all the different spectrums of healthcare. He has worked with patients in the intensive care unit all the way down to taking care of patients who are at end of life under hospice service.
